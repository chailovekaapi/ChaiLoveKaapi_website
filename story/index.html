Our Roots

Bridging the Gap.

We started with a simple observation: India loves its daily chai and kaapi, but the choice has always been between overpriced premium cafes or unhygienic roadside stalls.

Chai Love Kaapi was born to break that compromise. We deliver the authentic heritage taste of road-side filter coffee and masala chai, served in a spotless, modern, and highly systems-driven environment.

"Spotless or Shut"

This isn't just a marketing slogan; it's our core operational philosophy. Every Chai Love Kaapi outlet features an open-kitchen concept so you see exactly how your beverage is crafted.

  • 🧼
    Hourly Sanitization

    Counters and brassware are rigorously cleaned every hour.

  • 🧤
    Staff Protocols

    Gloves, hairnets, and strict hygienic handling are mandatory.

  • 📦
    Branded Precision Packaging

    Food-grade, spill-proof packaging ensures hygiene even on the go.
